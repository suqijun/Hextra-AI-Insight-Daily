### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file chillin' on your device. You can tell your browser to reject all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when a Cookie is being sent. But hey, if you don't play ball with Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain parts of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These bad boys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We totally use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its awesome features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have given the green light to using cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising through the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For more deets on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
